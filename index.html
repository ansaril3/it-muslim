Why us?

Higher quality

You get access to a second opinion, based on the experience of professional pathologists who trained Celly.AI algorithms. Our AI algorithms are constantly learning and their quality is always growing. It can operate with thousands of signs, which is impossible for humans.

Greater speed

On average, a pathologist spends a few minutes analysing and describing a single smear. AI processes the image in seconds. Machine does not get tired, it works 24/7.

Profitability

Convenient reports allow you to quickly make decisions, saving time (and hence money). High accuracy allows you to make correct diagnoses, recognize rare pathologies, which allows you to start treatment in time. The cost of the work of AI pathologist is much lower than that of an human specialist.

Unique AI training technology

To achieve the highest quality of neural networks training we use unique anti-CD antibody microarray technology which leads to highly accurate AI algorithms. Read more about the microarray technology in our Nature article.
(Left) The “map” of the microarray with numbers indicating the spots of mouse IgG against corresponding CD antigens; mIgG indicates the negative control; (middle left) the whole microarray with captured normal PBMC after May-Grünwald-Giemsa staining; (middle right and right) the anti-CD45-bound normal PBMC at different magnifications.

How it works

1

Upload

Physician uploads a smear image, to the system

2

Calculation

Within a few seconds Celly.AI counts cells, detects pathologies and marks the findings on the image

3

Diagnostic

Hematologist makes a diagnosis on the basis of a report, a clinical picture and his experience

4

Report

The system prepares a report, which can then be printed out or included in the patients medical card
